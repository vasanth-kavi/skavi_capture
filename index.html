About me

I'm Kavirayar , My hobby is photography.........,

A great benefit of photography as a hobby is that it can help improve your physical and mental health. As mentioned above, taking photos can be a great way to relax and unwind in an otherwise stressful world. It also gives you the opportunity to get out and explore new places, which can provide much-needed exercise.

In addition, photography teaches you patience when trying to capture the perfect picture, develops creative thinking and analytical skills, allows for self-expression through capturing what we see in the world around us, and provides a sense of accomplishment as our work improves over time with practice. All these factors contribute positively to our overall well-being.
